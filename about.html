Sobre o Foodfy

Founded in 2004 by Italian Italian Luca Lunghi, “La Bella Italia” is marked by a trajectory of constant evolution and innovation in the local gastronomic scene. With 100% Italian cuisine, the restaurant prides itself on rescuing the flavors of traditional cuisine combined with technique and innovation. The intention is to provide a trip to the affective memory of the flavors, textures and senses of authentic Italian cuisine.
